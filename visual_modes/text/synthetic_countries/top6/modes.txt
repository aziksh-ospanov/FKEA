Nature lovers will appreciate the diverse flora and fauna that call Tarija home, making it a paradise for eco-tourism in Bolivia.

Bolivia's diverse geography includes the Amazon rainforest, the Andes Mountains, and the Gran Chaco region. Tarija, nestled in the southern Andes, offers visitors a chance to explore the unique ecosystems of the area, sample regional cuisine, and interact with indigenous communities. The city of Tarija is a gateway to Bolivia's natural wonders.
